Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2025

youtube-tiktok-uploader-for-n8n is committed to protecting your privacy. This application is used to automate the uploading of videos to TikTok and YouTube using the n8n system.

Data Collection

This app does not store any personal data or user credentials permanently. OAuth tokens are used only temporarily during the upload process.

Use of Information

The tokens obtained are used exclusively to execute the video upload operation. No data is shared or sold to third parties.

Security

The app runs in a secure, private environment. Users are encouraged to keep their tokens and credentials safe.

Contact

For any questions regarding privacy, you may contact the developer via the GitHub repository or the associated email.
